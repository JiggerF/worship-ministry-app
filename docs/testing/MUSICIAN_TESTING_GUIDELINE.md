# Musician Testing Guideline: Song Card Transposer

**Goal:**
Ensure the “Change Key” feature correctly transposes chord charts for electric lead guitar, making them easy to read and play in any selected key.

---

## Steps

1. **Open the Song Card**
   - Select a song with chord charts available.

2. **Use the “Change Key” Feature**
   - Click the “Change Key” button.
   - Choose several different keys (e.g., C, D, E, F#, G, A, B).

3. **Download the Transposed Chart**
   - Use the “Download” button to save the PDF for each key you test.

4. **Review the Transposed Chords**
   - Open the PDF and check that all chords are correctly transposed to the selected key.
   - Compare the original chart and the transposed chart for accuracy.

5. **Play Through the Chart**
   - Play the song in the new key using the transposed chart.
   - Confirm that chord shapes and progressions make sense for electric guitar.

6. **Check Formatting**
   - Ensure chords are bold and colored, sections are clear, and lyrics are readable.

---

## What to Look For

- Are all chords transposed correctly for the selected key?
- Are any chords missing, incorrect, or oddly formatted?
- Is the PDF easy to read on your device?
- Does the chart match what you expect for electric lead guitar (no awkward shapes, progressions, or missing chords)?
- Is the “Change Key” feature fast and reliable?

---

## Feedback

- Note any errors, formatting issues, or usability problems.
- Suggest improvements for readability or guitar-specific needs.
