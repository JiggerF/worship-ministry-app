# App Personas & Roles

## 1. Admin
- **Access:** Full access to all features and settings.
- **Purpose:** Oversees the entire app, manages users, settings, and all content.

## 2. Worship Coordinator
- **Access:**
  - Login required
  - Full access to roster management (assign, draft, publish)
  - People page: read-only
  - Songs page: read-only
  - No access to settings
- **Purpose:** Manages team roster and scheduling, but cannot alter people or song data.

## 3. Music Coordinator
- **Access:**
  - Login required
  - Can add, edit, and approve songs
  - Can manage chord charts and song metadata
  - People and roster: read-only
  - No access to settings or user/team management
- **Purpose:** Maintains and curates the song library and music resources for the team.

## 4. Worship Leader
- **Access:**
  - Login required (future)
  - Can select songs for Sunday/setlists for their team/service
  - May have limited roster management for their own team
  - Can view and suggest songs
- **Purpose:** Prepares setlists and leads worship for specific services.

## 5. Musician
- **Access:**
  - No login required (public)
  - Can view setlists, chord charts, and resources
  - Cannot edit or manage any content
- **Purpose:** Accesses music and setlists for practice and performance, with no barriers to entry.

*This document is a living reference for all user personas and their permissions in the app. Update as new roles or requirements emerge.*
